Applying the Troubleshooting Process to Networking

After you have developed some theories about what is wrong, test your theories to determine the cause of the problem. Figure 3 is a list of quick procedures that can determine the exact cause of the problem or even correct the problem. If a quick procedure does correct the problem, you can then verify full system functionality. If a quick procedure does not correct the problem, you might need to research the problem further to establish the exact cause.
